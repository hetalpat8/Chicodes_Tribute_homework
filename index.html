Katherine G. Johnson

NASA

American mathematician(1918-2020)

10 Things you didn't know about Katherine G. Johnson:

  • She graduated from college at 18.
  • She was rejected by NASA the first time she applied.
  • She helped send astronaut Jogn Glenn into orbit.
  • She helped send the first astronaut to the moon.
  • She wrote a book on rocket science.
  • She contributed to plan for a mars mission.
  • She was given the presidential medal of freedom.
  • She received her doctorate 75 years after she dropped out of grad school.
  • Her life was made into a movie called Hidden Figures.
  • She was one of the first 3 AA students integrated into WV's graduate schools
